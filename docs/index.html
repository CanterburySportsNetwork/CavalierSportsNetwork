Welcome

The Cavalier Sports Network is a new and upcoming club that will update you and entertain you with all things sports related. This will include broadcasts, podcasts, articles, and more! We are excited to bring Cav sports more accessible to the Canterbury Community, and so friends, family, and relatives can tune in to your sports event through the broadcasts in our Youtube Channel. Please contact any leaders or members with questions, comments and concerns. Thank You!
